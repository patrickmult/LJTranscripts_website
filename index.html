What we do

Welcome to LJ Transcripts, a transcription service company! Since our establishment in 2000, we have been providing transcription and secretarial services to a variety of professionals and individuals including, but not limited to, attorneys, law enforcement agencies, private investigators, workers' compensation specialists, job analysts, nurse case managers, ergonomic evaluation specialists and private individuals. We offer proofreading services to a number of our clients as well.

How we help

Looking to cut costs? Feeling overwhelmed? Backlogged and buried in dictation? Wondering how you will get everything done and sent out on time? Try outsourcing to LJ Transcripts! If you are a business owner, office manager, private individual or a secretary who has a heavy workload or just needs help getting caught up, LJ Transcripts can provide transcription or remote admistrative assistant services and allow you the flexibility to focus on the more important tasks that require your attention.
